Vision, Mission & PEO's
Vision

To perpetually generate quality mechanical engineering technicians so as to cater the needs of industry and society.

Mission

1. To develop skilled and employable mechanical enginnering technicians by improved teaching learning process and industrial exposure.

2. To inculcate human values and engineering professional ethics in mechanical engineering technicians.

3. To develop attitude of self-learning and soft skills awareness in mechanical engineering technicians.
