Vision

" To Develop Competent IT Technician with Ethical Values for Betterment of Society. "

Mission

1. Provide socially responsible, environment friendly solutions to Information technology related broad-based problems adapting professional ethics.

3. Solve broad-based problems individually and as a team member communicating effectively in the world of work.
